THE CHALLENGE


As a university student balancing full-time studies and part-time work, maintaining consistent reading habits has become increasingly challenging. The core problem was the lack of an integrated platform that could:

  • Track reading progress across multiple platforms (Kindle, Audible, Libby, etc.)
  • Provide personalized book recommendations
  • Maintain reading motivation through social features
  • Set and track reading goals effectively

THE SOLUTION


StorySpark is a comprehensive reading companion app that integrates with popular reading platforms while adding social and gamification elements to keep readers engaged. Key features include:

Platform Integration

Seamless connection with Kindle, Audible, Libby, and Kobo

Smart Recommendations

Swipe-based book discovery system

Reading Timer

Daily tracking and goal setting

Social Challenges

Create and join reading challenges with friends
