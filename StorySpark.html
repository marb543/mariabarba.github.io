STORYSPARK 2024

I have been reading books from the time I was 14 , and it has been a long term passion of mine. However, I have always found myself struggling to maintain my reading habits throughout the school year. Because I am a university student full-time, and I also work part-time, I have trouble staying consistent with my reading goals.
I have designed a prototype of a reading app that would be able to integrate audio, and ebook platforms like Kindle, Audible, Libby , Kobo, etc , so that it can easily connect to my account , and keep track of my current reads. Furthermore, StorySpark would to motivate me to read the books that I will enjoy. In fact, the app has a swipe left/right recommendation system to track the books that I am reading, and it also hasa daily book reading timer. The app has a section of book reading challenges that I can create, and/or join with my friends or people that I meet online who are also part of the book community.
