ABOUT ME

Whether it is through programming, design, or art, I am a creator, a problem-solver, and a storyteller. Having worked in web development for more than two years, my area of expertise is creating user-friendly, intuitive web apps that combine design and functionality. My work flourishes at the nexus of creativity and technology, where I transform intricate concepts into dynamic digital representations.

In addition to working as a software developer, I have a strong enthusiasm for multimedia editing, visual storytelling, and painting. These elements motivate and stimulate my creativity and design process. Technology should be interesting, approachable, and meaningful in addition to being effective. My objective is always the same whether I'm creating a user interface or painting: to create, to innovate, and to leave a lasting impact.
